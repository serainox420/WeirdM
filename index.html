WeirdM

What is WeirdM?

WeirdM lets you create those weird WebM videos that resize as they play, from simple effects that work with any video to fully custom animations that you control keyframe by keyframe.

Everything is processed locally in your browser—your video never leaves your device. The source code is available on GitHub.
