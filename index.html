What is WeirdM?

WeirdM lets you create WebM files whose resolution changes over time. Choose a preset animation, randomise the chaos, design keyframes, or automatically trim away static borders. When you are ready, WeirdM hands everything to your local ffmpeg build.

  • Runs entirely on your machine—no uploads, no tracking.
  • Precise bounce, random, and timeline controls with easing options.
  • Smart trim for removing letterboxing or transparent borders.
  • Fine-tune CRF, frame rate, and audio passthrough before exporting.
